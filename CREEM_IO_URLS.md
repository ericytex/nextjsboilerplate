## How to Use in Creem.io

1. Go to your Creem.io dashboard
2. For each payment plan (Free, Basic, Pro, Business):
   - Find the "Success URL" or "Return URL" field
   - Paste the corresponding success URL above
   - Find the "Cancel URL" field
   - Paste: `https://nextjsboilerplate-bice.vercel.app/cancel`
3. Save your changes
